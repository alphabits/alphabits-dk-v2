About Alphabits

I started Alphabits in the summer 2007. In the beginning the focus was mainly on graphic design and web design. I pretty soon realised that my strength was in technical problem solving, and maybe not so much creative, visual problem solving, so as time has passed, Alphabits has become a software company.

It was never my intention to try to expand Alphabits, and maybe get one or more employees. Instead Alphabits has been my way, to work on exactly the jobs I wanted, at the time I wanted. It haven't made me rich, but I have learned a lot and it has always been entertaining.
